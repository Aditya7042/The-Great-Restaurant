About

The Great Restaurant is founded in 2023 by HIMANK ANAND

The Great Restaurant is a Contemporary Creole restaurant creating unique dishes with a focus on local ingredients and inspired by classical training.Located in Bhopal inNew Market, the main dining caters to the most unique dining experience. The interior is rich with original architectural details, gleaming hardwood floors, soaring columns and antique mirrors.The kitchen is overseen by Chef Lakshay dhawan.Our menu reflects a mix of italian, indian, chinese and american dishes
